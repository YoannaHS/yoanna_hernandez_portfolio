About Me

I am a passionate Product Owner and UX Leader with over 8 years of experience in digital product development. My expertise lies in bridging the gap between business objectives and user needs, creating products that deliver exceptional value.

With a background in both design and product management, I bring a unique perspective to every project. I believe in data-driven decision making, collaborative approaches, and continuous improvement.

My work spans across financial services, education, tourism, and technology sectors, where I've successfully led cross-functional teams to deliver innovative digital solutions.

Highlighted Projects

UX Challenge – BBVA

Led a comprehensive UX redesign for BBVA's mobile banking application, The bank aimed to improve foreign exchange simulation within the BBVA app. The intention was to increase the number of transactions carried out through this process and to establish it as the go-to digital option for currency exchange among users. I decided to conduct research using ChatGPT and Alsoasked, implementing AI and maximising technology to overcome the challenge. I successfully reduced research time by 80%.

UX Research IA ChatGPT Product Strategy

Talent Acquisition – Universidad del Pacífico

Education in crisis due to COVID-19: How did the pandemic impact university enrolments? Over the past three years, there has been a noticeable decline in the number of applications and university admissions, a situation exacerbated by the pandemic. Given this scenario, the goal was to increase enrolments, and research methods were implemented to determine the best way to reach high school students in their final years. As a result, we achieved a 40% increase in enrolments and admission processes.

How I Work

Stakeholder Management

Building strong relationships with all stakeholders to align business goals with user needs, ensuring clear communication and expectations throughout the project lifecycle.

UX Research

Conducting comprehensive user research to understand behaviors, needs, and motivations, using methodologies such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing to inform product decisions.

Product Definition

Translating research insights into clear product requirements, creating user stories, defining features, and establishing success metrics that align with both business objectives and user needs.

UX Design

Creating intuitive and engaging user experiences through wireframing, prototyping, and iterative design processes, focusing on usability, accessibility, and visual coherence.

Product Backlog Management

Prioritizing features and requirements based on business value and user impact, managing the product backlog, and collaborating with development teams to ensure successful implementation.
